This project analyzes how major economic and geopolitical events impact different stock sectors.

The motivation for this project comes from recent geopolitical tensions such as the U.S.–Iran conflict, which has led to a sharp rise in global energy prices. Oil prices surged rapidly due to supply disruptions and risks to key transit routes like the Strait of Hormuz. This created strong market movements, particularly in the energy sector, and triggered fear of missing out (FOMO) among investors.

This project aims to answer:

How do major economic/ geopolitical events impact different stock sectors? Do those impacts last or just short-term hype?
